Comfort zone

Quinten Clause

Next time you are looking at a pink and orange sunset, turn your gaze 180 degrees. You will see a deep cobalt blue sky with black shapes of clouds and buildings merging together. Artificial yellow dots tremble lightly. Perhaps you will see a silver moon that was sneaking behind your back, putting everything into a different light.

Next time you walk into a room and see all the chairs stacked on top of each other, it is a sign. No one was expecting your visit. Take note of the message. Maybe pick up the chairs, arrange them and wait for others to arrive. Make it yourself comfortable.
